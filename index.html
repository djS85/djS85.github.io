Website Instructions


Customer Use

To place an order, first you must enter your name and table number on the home page in the input boxes provided, this lets the staff know who you are and where you’re sitting so your order can be brought to the correct table.


After entering your details customer’s must then to go to the menu page by clicking the links provided on the home page, either on the navigation bar at the top or the ‘click here’ link below the input boxes.


After customers have entered detail’s they can then start adding items to their order. On the menu page items can be added by just clicking the ‘Add’ button next to an item or, items can be added by quantity to the order.


To edit the order customer’s must click on the ‘View Order’ link on the right-hand side of the navbar, this shows a breakdown of the customer’s order and allows items to be removed, or allows customers to delete their entire order and start again by clicking the red ‘Delete Order’ button on the right=hand side of the navbar.


Once a customer is happy with their order, they must be on the view order page and click the green ‘Submit Order’ button to place and finalize their order.

Admin Use

An admin user, possibly a staff member or manager, has access to the ‘Admin Home’ page which is accessible from the main home page by clicking the ‘Admin Home’ button at the right-hand side of the navbar. This takes the user to the Admin Home page with 2 options; ‘View Orders’, where all orders are displayed by date, from today’s date in descending order, and ‘View Products’, which shows all the products available, split into 2 tables of food and drink.


View Orders

Below the list of orders is an input box that can be used to enter an order number and view the items on the order, the quantity of each item, each items price per unit and the total cost of the order.


Product View

This page allows the admin user to view and edit the products table of the database, here products can be deleted or updated by their product ID, shown in the tables, or an entirely new product can be added to the database. Editing the products table is done by filling in the appropriate input boxes at the bottom of the page and clicking the corresponding button.
